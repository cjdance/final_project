Significant Strikes Landed per Minute (SLpM)

SLpM

Significant strikes landed per minute is a measure of how many strikes at distance and power strikes in the clinch and on the ground a fighter lands on their opponent per minute of fight time.

Significant Strikes Absorbed per Minute (SApM)

SApM

Significant strikes absorbed per minute is a measure of how many strikes at distance and power strikes in the clinch and on the ground a fighter is hit with by their opponent per minute of fight time.

Significant Strikes Landed Percentage (SL%)

SL%

Significant strikes landed percentage is the percentage of significant strikes a fighter lands based on the total they attempt in their fights.


Significant Strikes Defended Percentage (SD%)

SD%

Significant strikes landed percentage is the percentage of significant strikes a fighter blocks or avoids based on the total their opponents have thrown at them.

Takedowns per 15 minutes (TDp15M)

TDp15M

Takedowns per 15 minutes is a measure of the number of completed takedowns a fighter averages in 15 minutes of fight time.
A takedown is defined as a fighter forcing their opponent off their feet and achieving a measure of control which prevents them from rising immediately to their feet again.

Takedown Accuracy (TDacc)

TDacc

Takedown accuracy is a percentage measure of the number of successful takedowns a fighter has achieved out of their total takedown attempts.


Submission Average per 15 Minutes (SubAVG)

SubAVG

Submission average is a measure of the number of submission attempts a fighter averages per 15 minutes of fight time.

Stance

Stance

Stance is a label for the foot/hand which a fighter leads with when fighting. Orthodox fights have their left hand and foot forward, Southpaw have their right hand and foot forward.

Record

(W-L-D)

This is a measure of the fighter's record in previous combat sports events. "W" is the number of wins a fighter has, "D" is draws (i.e. no winner) and "L" is the number of losses they have incurred.
